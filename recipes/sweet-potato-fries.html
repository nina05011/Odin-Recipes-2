Sweet Potato Fries

The perfect healthy snack!

Craving some fries but trying your best to eat clean? These fries are the answer for you! Ready in under 30 minutes, these tasty fries will help you smash that craving for some salty goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatos
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 2 table spoons olive oil

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit.
  2. Wash, peel and cut sweet potatoes into 1/2 fries.
  3. Place fries on baking sheet with parchment paper. Coat with olive oil and condiments, toss to combine.
  4. Place in oven for 22mins, flipping halfway through.
