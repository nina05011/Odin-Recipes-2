Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious, goey and, most importantly, easy to make cookies!

This recipes is for all the sweet tooths out there! With ingredients most of you will likely find in your pantry already, this is a quick and tasty treat for the whole family to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup 1/4 flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until fluffy, then add in egg.
  3. Add in peanut butter and vanilla extract
  4. In a seperate bowl sift all the dry ingredients together.
  5. Gradually add dry mixture into wet mixture, continuously mixing.
  6. Add chocolate chips to the batter.
  7. Roll batter into walnut sized balls. Bake in oven for 12-15mins, until edges are golden brown.
